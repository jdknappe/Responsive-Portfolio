Bio

Daft Punk are a French electronic music duo formed by Guy-Manuel Homem-Christo and Thomas Bangalter in 1993. Together, they achieved great success and popularity in the late 1990s as part of the French House movement and have enjoyed continuing success years after. Their style of music combines elements of house music with funk, rock, techno, disco, and synthpop influences. Daft Punk's most recognizable contributions in recent years include their latest album, "Random Access Memories", featuring their lead single titled, "Get Lucky", which was an international success peaking on top 10 charts in 32 countries.

Daft Punk are mainly recognized by their 'robotic' appearance, with Thomas Bangalter sporting a silver/chrome helmet and Guy-Manuel with a helmet featuring a gold/brass-like appearance. They very rarely show their faces in videos and television interviews. Because of this, the mystery of their identity and the added complexity behind their disguises have contributed to their popularity.
